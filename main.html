Mind Facts

What is anxiety?

Anxiety is your brain's overprotective alarm system. It's designed to keep you safe, but sometimes it misfires, making you feel threatened when there's no real danger.

Stress vs. Burnout

Stress is acute pressure that comes and goes. Burnout is chronic exhaustion from prolonged stress. While stress feels like drowning, burnout feels like being all dried up.

Cognitive Distortions

Your brain sometimes lies through patterns like "all-or-nothing" thinking or catastrophizing. Recognizing these distortions is the first step to challenging them.

Brain & Emotions

Your brain processes emotions in the limbic system, while rational thought happens in the prefrontal cortex. This is why emotions can feel so powerful and overwhelming.
